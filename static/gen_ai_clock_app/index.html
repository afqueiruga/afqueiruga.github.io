About the App

GenAI Clock is an Apple Watch and iPhone app that displays AI-generated clock face imagery. It is an art project that explores what happens when generative AI attempts precision tasks like rendering clock faces.

A custom ML algorithm ensures the displayed time is always correct, but the artwork around it is intentionally imperfect: hands sprout extra segments, numerals drift into impossible positions, and dials twist into surreal shapes.

Platforms:

Apple Watch iPhone iPad

Requires: watchOS 11.5 or later, iOS 18.5 or later

Features

  • A new AI-generated clock face every minute you glance at your wrist
  • Multiple visual styles to choose from
  • Watch complication for glanceable generative art on your watch face
  • iPhone and iPad app with home screen and Lock Screen widgets
  • World Clock widget showing AI clock faces for multiple timezones
  • Digital Crown scrolls through time for previewing other clock faces
  • Fully offline — all images are pre-loaded on device
  • No data collection, no accounts, no subscriptions

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do the clock faces look wrong?

That's the point! GenAI Clock is an art project that highlights how generative AI struggles with precision tasks. The time shown is always accurate, but the artwork is intentionally imperfect.

Does this replace my Apple Watch face?

No. GenAI Clock is a standalone app and does not modify or replace your system watch face. The watch complication displays AI-generated art within Apple's standard complication slots.

How do I add the complication to my watch face?

Long-press your watch face, tap Edit, swipe to the complications section, tap a rectangular slot, and select GenAI Clock from the list.

How do I add widgets on iPhone or iPad?

Long-press your home screen, tap the + button, search for GenAI Clock, and choose from small, medium, or large sizes. A World Clock widget is also available.

How do I change styles?

On Apple Watch, long-press the clock face in the app to open Settings, then enable or disable styles. On iPhone, open the app and go to Settings to select your preferred styles.

How do I scroll through time on the watch?

Turn the Digital Crown to preview different minutes. The display automatically returns to the current time after 10 seconds.

The widget appears blank or stuck.

Widgets rely on the system's WidgetKit framework, which has aggressive caching. Try these steps:

  • Restart your Apple Watch or iPhone
  • Remove the widget/complication and re-add it
  • Make sure GenAI Clock is installed (not offloaded)

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. All clock face images are stored locally on your device. The app works entirely offline.
