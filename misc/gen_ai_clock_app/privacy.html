This Privacy Policy describes how GenAI Clock ("the App") handles your information.

Data Collection

GenAI Clock does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The App operates entirely offline and does not communicate with any external servers.

What the App Does Not Do

  • Does not collect personal information of any kind
  • Does not require or support user accounts or sign-in
  • Does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks
  • Does not access your contacts, photos, camera, microphone, location, or health data
  • Does not use cookies or similar technologies

On-Device Storage

The App stores a small amount of preference data locally on your device, such as your selected visual styles and timezone settings. This data is stored using standard system storage, never leaves your device, and is not accessible to the developer or any third party. You can remove all app data by deleting the App from your device.

Third-Party Services

GenAI Clock does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because no data is collected, there is no risk of inadvertent collection of children's data.

App Store Privacy Labels

In accordance with the App Store, we have declared "Data Not Collected" for all data categories in our App Privacy details.

Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Because the App collects no data, material changes are unlikely.
